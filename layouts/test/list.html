Who We Are

In 2026, Roppongi Venture Capital was founded on a single conviction: Japan's brightest STEM minds deserve world-class entrepreneurial experiences. We identify Math and Science Olympiad medalists, nurture them through a 6-month incubation program, and send the top performers to the World Economic Forum in Davos.

We pioneered the upstream model — rather than sourcing deal flow from existing founders, we create the founders ourselves. From incubation to venture investment, from deep tech decoded to data journalism, our flywheel turns talent into companies and companies into returns.

We invest in deep tech companies across quantum computing, nuclear fusion, synthetic biology, advanced materials, and AI/robotics. We believe the future belongs to builders, and our job is to make sure they have what they need to build it.

Our culture is our way of doing things, not just reading about them. We are clear about our beliefs and what we stand for.

Traditional VCs wait for founders to come to them. We go upstream. We identify Japan's most gifted STEM students — Math Olympiad medalists, Science Olympiad finalists — and give them the experiences, mentorship, and global exposure that transforms raw talent into entrepreneurial ambition.

Our 6-month incubation program culminates in a trip to Davos, where participants meet heads of state, Nobel laureates, and global CEOs. The scale of that experience, at that age, is transformative.

Our relationship with founders begins years before they start a company. We build trust through education, mentorship, and genuine investment in their growth as people — not just as potential portfolio companies.

Japan has 16 unicorns. The US has 780. The gap is not about technology — it's about the ecosystem that turns technology into businesses. We exist to close that gap, starting from the very top of the talent pipeline.
